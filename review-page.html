Case Study: Creating an Online Review Platform for Unique Furniture Kenya

  • Client: Unique Furniture Kenya
  • Project: Development of an Online Review Page
  • Objective: Enhance customer feedback collection by shifting from offline to an online review system, improving customer experience and boosting sales team credibility.

  • Client Name: Unique Furniture Kenya

  • Project Category: Ui/Ux Design, Wordpress

  • Project Date: 20 Jan 2025

  • Open External Link

Unique Furniture Kenya had been relying on offline customer feedback, primarily collected through in-store interactions. However, the sales team reported that many clients preferred an online review platform where they could share their experiences conveniently.


Based on this feedback, I initiated and executed the development of an Online Review Page, ensuring a seamless experience for both customers and sales representatives.


1. Wireframe Design


  • A clean and user-friendly interface
  • Easy navigation to different sales representatives' pages
  • A simple review submission form
  • Displaying previous customer reviews for transparency and trust-building

2. High-Fidelity Design


Once the wireframe was approved, I created a high-fidelity prototype incorporating:

  • Brand-aligned colors and typography
  • A structured design ensuring intuitive user interaction
  • Mobile responsiveness to cater to all device users
